About this map

Every building footprint in Pulaski County, Arkansas, colored by assessor data. Inspired by Colouring London and the Amsterdam building-age map.

Data sources

  • Building footprints & parcels — PAgis (Pulaski Area GIS)
  • Year built, stories, size, type — Pulaski County Assessor CAMA raw export
  • Vehicles & personal property — Pulaski County Assessor personal property export (public assessment records), matched to buildings by situs address; counts reflect each account's most recent assessment

Caveats

  • Assessor attributes describe the parcel's primary improvements. Every structure on a parcel inherits them, so a 2005 garage next to a 1940 house shows 1940. Use "main buildings only" to hide accessory structures.
  • About 10% of footprints (rights-of-way, common areas, exempt land, new splits) have no assessor match and are shown as "undated / unknown".
  • Vehicle counts are summed per street address: apartment complexes aggregate all residents' vehicles, dealers/leasing lots can reach hundreds, and every building on a parcel shows its parcel's totals. Business equipment is excluded from vehicle counts but included in personal-property value.
  • Unofficial visualization; not an authoritative record.
